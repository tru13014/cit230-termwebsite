Leverich Canyon Trail Tour

Leverich Canyon Trail is a moderately trafficked trail located near Bozeman, Montana, that features beautiful wild flowers, breath-taking mountain views, and is rated as moderate. The trail offers a number of activity options and is best used from May until September5. Details:

  • Intense climb, intense descent, great group fun
  • Who: Intermediate riders
  • Distance: 4.9 miles
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Elevation gain: 1269 feet
  • Route type: Loop
  • Price $140 per person
  • Location: 3rd Avenue, Bozeman,MT

Sypes Canyon Trail Tour

Popular trail just north of Bozeman, Montana, near the southern end of the Bridger Range. The hike leads through a shaded forest following a creek-fed canyon with an overlook of the expansive Gallatin Valley at 2 miles and of Mount Baldy, the prominent 8,914-foot summit. Details:

  • Moderate climb and descent, great family fun
  • Who: Beginner to Intermediate riders
  • Distance: 4 miles
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Elevation gain: 1000 feet
  • Route type: Loop
  • Price: $120 per person
  • Location: Sypes Canyon Rd., Bozeman,MT

Snow Biking Tours

Snow bike tours are only available Sunday through Thursday during the Winter months. All snow bike tours are guided and for all ages. There are teambuilding tours available, contact us for specific dates and times, as they vary each season, based on weather conditions.6. Details:

  • Moderate to intense climb, for the serious biker
  • Who: Experience riders
  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Distance: From 4 to 10 miles
  • Elevation gain: Up to 10,000 feet
  • Route type: Multiple trails
  • Price: $220 per person
  • Location: Vary, contact us for details
